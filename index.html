About

My name is Adit Gupta, and I am a student of the College of Computing and Informatics at Drexel university, studying Software Engineering. I have been recognized as an Undergraduate Research Leader, Star Scholar, and Microsoft Student Partner. As a head organizer of Philly Codefest, I have helped organize an event with over 400 talented computer scientists from all over the country. My hobbies include developing innovative ideas with new technologies, going to hackathons,and teaching and playing tennis at a competitive level. I have been teaching tennis from the age of 12, and since then I have maintained and satisfied over 65 clients. I love learning and giving back to my community. I have an insatiable hunger to improve humanity and reduce suffering using technology. My goal is to use my communication skills, entrepreneurial acumen, and technical experience to create a company to further advance humanity.

Entrepreneurship

I am the recipient of Drexel's prestigious Dragon Leadership Scholarship. As an aspiring technical entrepreneur, I travel around the world to attend Hackathons. As the founder and president of the Dragon Hackers Club, our goal is to ensure a safe environment for creators to explore technologies and participate in educational events to represent Drexel University.

Computer Science

I am currently enrolled in junior-level, Software Engineering courses at Drexel University and am proficient in Java, Javascript, C#, HTML, CSS,with a working familiarity with C/C++ and Python. I completed a full-time internship at Susquehanna International Group, and I am a Microsoft Student Partner, 1 of 150 scholars chosen from a pool of over 4000 applicants.

Communication

I have demonstrated the ability to convey complex, important information to wide audiences. As an active member of Toast Masters I have learned to speak to a wide variety of audience. As an active member of the technical and entrepreneurial community, I host workshops for college students, with the purpose to help advance their soft skills and technical knowledge.
